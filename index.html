Vent · Live product · Early traction

People meet through what they are going through—not who they pretend to be.

Vent is a context-first peer connection network. A user chooses a specific topic, states what kind of conversation they need, sends or accepts a request and enters a focused 24-hour conversation by mutual consent.

The gap

Social media optimizes visibility. Forums are slow. Therapy is not always available and Vent does not replace it. Vent is built for the moment someone simply needs the right human being now.

Forge · Product beta · Pre-traction

A global network that turns the intention to move into real-world action.

Forge connects health and fitness discovery with nearby people, public places, events, professionals and commerce. It is English-first and global by design.

Built in Istanbul. Designed for the world.

Istanbul is our first density market—not our geographic limit.

It is where I can manually observe supply, behavior and local activation. From day one of the partner launch, a verified business in Los Angeles, Munich or anywhere else will be able to join remotely and create its own Forge node.

Partner-led global expansion

Every partner can become a local growth node.

Forge does not need a physical sales team in every city. From launch, any verified sports, fitness or wellness business will be able to join remotely, fill its profile and storefront, then activate its existing community with a localized QR and social media kit.
